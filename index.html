About

This is a real showcase of a Cisco IoT solution: Getting sensor data via LoRaWAN to display its information on a website and via a Webex Teams bot. It is currently in use in the Cisco office in Frankfurt, Germany for owners of electric vehicles to check if available e-parking spots (with charging stations) are available.

Contact: Florian Pachinger (flpachin), Michael Eder (miceder)
