This is a real showcase of a Cisco IoT solution

Getting sensor data via LoRaWAN to display its information on this website and via a Webex Teams bot. It is currently in use in the Cisco office in Frankfurt, Germany for owners of electric vehicles to check if available e-parking spaces (with charging stations) are available.
